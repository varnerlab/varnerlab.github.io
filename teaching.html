Financial Data, Markets, and Mayhem for Scientists and Engineers

Teaches scientists and engineers to make quantitative financial decisions in corporate and wealth-management contexts, applying engineering, statistics, AI, and machine learning. Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Methods for Engineers
